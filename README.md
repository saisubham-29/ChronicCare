<!-- ================= HEADER ================= -->
<h1 align="center">🩺 ChronicCare – Chronic Disease Tracking App</h1>

<p align="center" style="max-width:760px; margin:18px auto; line-height:1.7; font-size:15px;">
  <b>ChronicCare</b> is an Android application designed to assist individuals living with
  chronic diseases by tracking medical bio-data in real time and acting as a
  <b>virtual health companion</b>.
</p>

<hr/>

<!-- ================= ABOUT ================= -->
<h2>📌 About the Project</h2>

<p style="line-height:1.7;">
  This application was developed by a team of three contributors using a
  <b>UI mockup and design showcase as reference</b>.
  The focus of the project is on <b>application development, feature implementation,
  system logic, and user experience</b>.
</p>

<p style="line-height:1.7;">
  ChronicCare aims to improve health awareness by helping users understand
  their medical data, medications, and health implications in a simplified
  and supportive manner.
</p>

<!-- ================= FEATURES ================= -->
<h2>✨ Key Features</h2>

<hr/>

<!-- ================= AI FEATURE ================= -->
<h2>🤖 Dr.GPT – AI Medical Assistance (Assistive)</h2>

<p style="line-height:1.7;">
  <b>Dr.GPT</b> is an AI-powered feature integrated into ChronicCare that provides
  <b>suggestive and conversational assistance</b> to help users better understand
  medical reports, medications, and general health information.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>💬 Conversational AI for health-related queries</li>
  <li>📄 Simplifies complex medical reports</li>
  <li>💊 Helps users understand medications and implications</li>
  <li>🧠 Awareness-focused guidance</li>
</ul>

<p style="line-height:1.7;">
  <b>Important:</b> Dr.GPT does <b>not replace a doctor</b>.
  It does not provide diagnosis or treatment decisions and is intended
  only to support understanding and awareness.
</p>

<p style="line-height:1.7;">
  Users are advised <b>not to rely on Dr.GPT for medical decisions</b> and
  to consult qualified healthcare professionals.
</p>
